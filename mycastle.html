mycastle

Mycastle adds blocks for building castles.



There is a couple sizes of drawbridges you can use. Right click them and they go up or down.



There's many blocks to use.



There are fireplaces. Right click on them to light the flame or put it out.



There is even furniture for you to use.



I hope you find everything you need to build your castle!
