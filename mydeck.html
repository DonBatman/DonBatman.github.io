mydeck


mydeck is a mod for Luanti that allows you to add realistic looking decks. To build them craft a machine and make the parts.





You can stain the deck. You need to craft a stain brush and then mix the brush with dye. Once you have the stain brush with dye just click on what you want stained and it happens.
