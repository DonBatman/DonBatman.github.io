myroofs

myroofs adds sloped roof to Luanti. These are meshnode slopes so it gives you a nice smooth roof.There's a number of styles and colors to pick from.



First you need to craft a bundle of shingles.






With the bundles you can now make your roof parts.




You can also make chimneys and chimney tops.
