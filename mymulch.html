mymulch


mymulch allows you to use grass, sticks, leaves and other plants to make mulch. You can color the mulch and use it in your garden. Plants and trees will grow in it.
You need to craft a composter first.



Once you have the composter right click on it. In the top left add leaves sticks and other plants and flowers to make mulch. Add dye to make colored mulch. On the right you can put the mulch in the top and it will slowly become clay. In the bottom left you can turn the mulch into dirt.
