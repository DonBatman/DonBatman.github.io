mymeshnodes

mymeshnodes allows you to make a lot of different shapes in Luanti. They are nice smooth meshnodes. Most of the models were done by VanessaE or ExeterDad. Max Cohen worked on getting more mesh nodes made. These nodes make building more exciting.

The latest addition is the 45 degree slope. It allows you to make slopes on a 45 degree angle. Thanks Max for that. They are awesome! I also added half blocks. They are nodeboxes but have been a part of noncubic for a while so I thought I would throw them in.
