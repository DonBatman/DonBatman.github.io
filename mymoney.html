mymagic


Complete economy mod ðŸ”—
Collect gold and silver and then turn them into coins. Use the coins at shops or vending machines.

Making Money ðŸ”—
When you go mining you collect gold. If you have myores installed then you can also collect silver. Craft a coin maker and place the gold or silver in it. It automatically turns the ore into money over time.

Spending Money ðŸ”—
There are admin shops that the server owner can set up to sell items. People can craft a shop to sell things. There is also a vending machine you can craft to sell items.

If you have myland installed you can buy land from people.

Saving Money ðŸ”—
Instead of keeping all your coins in your inventory you can craft a wallet. The wallet holds your money and only takes up one spot in your inventory.

If your wallet is getting full it might be time to put your money in the bank. Find an ATM. You need to have your bank card in your inventory to use the bank machine. Putyour money in the bank and save up for the big item you have your eyes on.

Trading Items ðŸ”—
Since you can't always trust people and you never know who you can trust you need a way to trade items safely. The trade table does just that. Put your item in and they put thiers. You can not take an item until you both agree and then the trade is done.

Doing Odd Jobs ðŸ”—
Do you want some items? Don't feel like going to get them? Set up a job board. Set it up for what you want and how much you will pay. People see the job board and go do the work for you. If you are low on money you can find jobs to do and get them done.

Finding Money ðŸ”—
Adventure around the world. You might find a chest full of money!
