mymineshaft


My Mineshaft lets you make a cool shaft for mining. Dig a hole straight down. Place a bottom and then start placing shaft all the way up. At the top you can either leave it an open shaft or put a top on it. The top opens and closes when you punch it. Just make a machine to get started.



Right click on the machine to start making the parts.
