mysiding

mysiding adds lines to the front of the block. All other sides stay the same. Simply craft the machine. Open the machine and put your blocks in. Click either wide or narrow and take your blocks. When the blocks are placed the side with the lines will face you. There are also corner blocks.


Here is the craft for the machine


Place your material in the machine and click on the style you want. The dark lines are good for lighter blocks and darker blocks should have the lighter lines.
