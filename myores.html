myores


Adds several stone,ores and minerals. They include;

  • slate
  • shale
  • schist
  • gneiss
  • basalt
  • granite
  • marble
  • chromium
  • manganese
  • chalk
  • calcium
  • sodium
  • silver
  • quartz
  • chalcopyrite
  • cobalt
  • uvarovite
  • selenite
  • miserite
  • limonite
  • sulfur
  • lapis lazuli
  • emerald
  • amethyst




It is supported by mywalls, mybricks, mysiding, myflooring, mystonepaths and myholeinthewall.
