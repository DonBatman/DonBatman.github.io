mylights


Mylights is a mod for Luanti for crafting lights and light bulbs. You make light bulbs of different watts. When you craft a light the brightness is dependent on the wattage of the bulb you used. There are 30 watt, 60 watt, 90 watt and 120 watt bulbs.



You need to craft a machine to make light bulbs.


To make light boxes you need a different machine.
